Get In Touch

Whether you have questions, suggestions, feedback, or need technical support, we're here to help. Your input helps us improve PDF Genius Tools and create better tools for everyone.

Contact Information

  • General Support: support@pdfgeniustools.online
  • Business Inquiries: partnerships@pdfgeniustools.online
  • Hours: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (EST)

Technical Support

For technical issues or bug reports, please include the following information to help us resolve your issue faster:

  • The tool you were using (e.g. Merge PDF, Compress PDF)
  • Your browser name and version (e.g. Chrome 120)
  • Your operating system (e.g. Windows 11, macOS 14)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Any error messages you saw

Feature Requests & Suggestions

We welcome ideas for new tools or improvements to existing ones. If you have a suggestion, please send it to our support email with the subject line "Feature Request". Your feedback directly shapes the future of PDF Genius Tools.

Business & Partnership Inquiries

For partnership opportunities, enterprise solutions, white-label options, or media inquiries, please reach out to us at partnerships@pdfgeniustools.online. We typically respond within 2 business days.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1–2 business days. For urgent technical issues that prevent you from using a tool, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.
