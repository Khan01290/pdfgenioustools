Privacy Policy

Learn how PDF Genius Tools protects your privacy. All file processing is done in your browser — your files never leave your device.

🔒 Your Privacy is Our Priority

1. Introduction

At PDF Genius Tools, we prioritize your privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free online PDF tools. Since all file processing occurs directly in your browser, we have designed our service to be fundamentally private by architecture — your documents never leave your device.

2. No File Uploads

Unlike many online PDF services, we never transfer your files to our servers. When you use any tool on PDF Genius Tools:

  • Files remain exclusively on your device
  • All processing occurs within your web browser
  • No copies are stored on any server
  • We have zero access to your document content

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We intentionally avoid collecting:

  • File contents or document metadata
  • Personal identifiers or user profiles
  • Browsing history within our tools
  • Location data or device identifiers

4. Analytics Usage

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us understand tool popularity, identify performance issues, and measure feature adoption. This data is completely anonymized and contains no personal information or file contents. We have configured Google Analytics to respect "Do Not Track" browser signals.

5. Email Communications

When you contact us via email:

  • We retain your email address only for response purposes
  • Correspondence is stored for up to 2 years
  • We never add you to marketing lists without explicit consent
  • You may request deletion of correspondence at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for essential functions only:

  • Session cookies to remember your dark/light theme preference
  • First-party Google Analytics cookies (anonymized)
  • No third-party advertising or tracking cookies

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Security Measures

While your files never leave your device, we implement the following security measures on our platform:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Regular security audits of our codebase
  • Subresource Integrity (SRI) checks for third-party scripts
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers

9. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to any information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your information, and object to processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@pdfgeniustools.online.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced through website notices and updated revision dates at the bottom of this page.
